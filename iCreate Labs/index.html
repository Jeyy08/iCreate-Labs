About iCreateLab

“ Empowering Innovation, Fostering Growth Welcome to iCreateLab, an ecosystem meticulously crafted to empower ICT-focused tech entrepreneurs and young individuals through the integration of STEM pedagogy. At iCreateLab, we believe in the transformative power of technology and innovation as catalysts for positive change. At the core of iCreateLab's mission is the vision of fostering an environment where innovative minds can thrive and create impactful solutions. We are dedicated to building a framework that enables the development of groundbreaking solutions, enterprises, and companies to address society's most pressing challenges. iCreateLab is more than just a company; we are architects of an innovation ecosystem. Our focus is on providing a nurturing environment for startups, where creativity meets technology, and ideas evolve into tangible solutions. We understand the unique challenges faced by tech entrepreneurs, and our ecosystem is designed to provide comprehensive support. STEMLAB, the genesis of iCreateLab, was established with a grand vision—to empower, promote, and position Africa, particularly Nigeria, at the forefront of technology and the startup ecosystem. Our vision extends beyond business; it's a commitment to leveraging technology as a strategic tool to combat unemployment and drive sustainable development”
